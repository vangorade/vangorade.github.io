Vandan Gorade

I am Ph.D. candidate at Biomedical Engineering Department at Northwestern University, where I am a member of the Machine and Hybrid Intelligence Lab, advised by Prof. Ulas Bagci. My research interests broadly lie in the applications of deep learning in the Medical Image Analysis domain. Currently, I am working on foundational models for medical applications.

Before starting my graduate studies, I worked as an affiliate researcher with Prof. Ulas Bagci and Dr. Debesh Jha at Northwestern University. I have also served as a researcher at the Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee, working with Dr. Sparsh Mittal, and the Indian Institute of Technology, Jodhpur, with Dr. Deepak Mishra. Additionally, I spent time as a associate researcher at the Reliance Jio Institute, working with Dr. Sudipta Roy. During my undergrad, I also gained industry experience at multiple ML startups.
